Information

Gallery Limbus is an online 3D gallery available through the browser on your laptop or computer - no downloads or installations required and completely covid-free. Press the two arrows in the bottom right corner to enter fullscreen and use Esc to exit. Use the W, A, S and D buttons to walk (or alternatively the arrow keys) and use your mouse to look around. Use shift to move faster. The experience uses sound, so be sure to use speakers or headphones before entering the gallery. Welcome to Gallery Limbus.

NOTE: Having issues displaying the gallery in your browser? Make sure to enable WebGL. Press here to learn how to do that. Alternatively, try a different browser or computer. If your cursor gets stuck while moving, use an actual mouse instead of a touchpad. If the gallery lags, use a stronger computer or exit fullscreen to view the gallery in a windowed mode.

Find more art at: https://www.raudart.com/
